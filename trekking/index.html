I love to trek, especially in Himalaya. It started in 2005 with a pilgrimage trek to Gomukh, Kedarnath, and Badrinath with family. I have been to following treks:

  • 2008: Gomukh, Tapovan, Nanadanvan & Vasuki Taal
  • 2010: From Gangotri to Kedarnath via Kedar Taal, Patangani Dhar, Auden's Col & Mayali Pass
  • 2012: Unsuccessful Kalindi Pass attempt
  • 2013: Pin - Parvati Pass
  • 2015: Roopkund, Hemkund, crossing Ronti Saddle pass to Bhavishya Badri
